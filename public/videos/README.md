# Video Instructions

## Hero Background Video

Place your hero background video here as: `hero-background.mp4`

### Video Specifications:
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD) or 3840x2160 (4K)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
- **Duration:** 10-30 seconds (will loop)
- **File Size:** Recommended < 10MB (optimized)
- **FPS:** 24-30 fps
- **Bitrate:** 2-5 Mbps

### Optimization Tips:
1. Use Handbrake or Adobe Media Encoder to compress
2. Keep it short (10-20 seconds) for faster loading
3. Add a poster image at `/public/images/hero-poster.jpg` for the first frame
4. Test on mobile devices for performance

### Content Suggestions:
- Sizzling shawarma on the grill
- Chef preparing food
- Close-up of delicious dishes
- Restaurant ambiance
- Food being served

Once you add your video, it will automatically loop in the background of the hero section.
